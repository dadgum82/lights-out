# Lights Out

A classic Lights Out puzzle game.

## Overview

Lights Out is a TypeScript-based puzzle game where the goal is to turn off all the lights.

## How to Play

1. Click on a light to toggle it and its neighbors
2. Try to turn off all lights to win
